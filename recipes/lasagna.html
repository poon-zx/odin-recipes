Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the name of one of the oldest and best-known pasta shapes. It is usually rectangular or ribbon shaped, thicker than tagliatelle, made from a dough based on flour and eggs.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
